Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to some of the most common questions about my services and process.

How do I get a quote for a service?

The best way to get a quote is to contact me directly through the "Contact" form or by phone. We can discuss your specific needs, and I'll provide a detailed, no-obligation estimate tailored to your project.

What is your process for web development projects?

My process typically starts with a consultation to understand your vision and goals. From there, I'll propose a design and plan, build the website, and provide support until it's live. I focus on creating modern, mobile-friendly, and easy-to-manage sites.

Can you improve my existing Wi-Fi network's coverage?

Yes, absolutely. I can perform a site survey to identify weak signal areas and recommend the best solutions, such as installing a mesh network, signal boosters, or additional access points to ensure fast and reliable connectivity in every corner of your home or office.

What kind of CCTV and security systems do you install?

I install a variety of security systems, from single-camera setups for small homes to multi-camera surveillance systems for businesses. I work with both wired and wireless solutions and can integrate features like motion detection, remote viewing, and cloud storage.

Do you offer maintenance or support after installation?

Yes, I offer ongoing support for all my clients. If you have any questions or need help with your newly installed Wi-Fi, CCTV, or web development project, you can contact me for assistance.
